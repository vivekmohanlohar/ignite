Eligibility Criteria


The contest is open for B.E/B. Tech and Polytechnic level students.

Rules & Regulations


    Solo and Group Dance


  • Track limit should be 4min (Exceeding the time may lead to deduction in marks)

  • Duration of performance: 4min

  • It’s mandatory to specify the Theme

  • Song Content should be relevant to theme.

  • Participant may submit only one song with registration (per person). The category for the song has to be selected during registration.

  • Use of Vulgar/Violent language or Lyrics is strictly prohibited.

  • Contestant should be dressed appropriately during the performance.

  • If a participant is found violating any of these rules, it would lead to the disqualification of the participant, who would also be required to leave the premises immediately.

  • Judgment is based on basis of rhythm, formation, expressions, costumes, makeup, sets on the overall effect.

  • - Rules listed are subject to change as per the discretion of the Event Heads and the Organizing Committee

  • The decision of the Organizing Committee shall be final in all matters.

    Gruop Dance / Duet Dance


  • Its mandatory to Specify “Group name” as well as “Theme”

  • Track limit should be maximum of 4 to 5min.

  • Group participant should be minimum of 2 and maximum of 8.

  • Song Content should be relevant to theme.

  • Use of Vulgar/Violent language or Lyrics is strictly prohibited.

  • Contestant should be dressed appropriately during the performance.

  • The Judging criteria for the participants will be as follows:

  • Choreography, Music, Coordination,Adherence to, and depiction of the theme provided, Rhythm, Walk of each participant, Overall impact of the performance

  • Rules listed are subject to change as per the discretion of the Event Heads and the Organizing Committee.

  • The decision of the Organizing Committee shall be final in all matters.

  • On the day of performance, participants must check their sequence no. and have to present accordingly at the back of the stage.
