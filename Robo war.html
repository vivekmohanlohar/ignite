ROBO WAR

Price


    1st price 3000

    2nd price 2000

Eligibility Criteria


The contest is open for B.E/B. Tech and Polytechnic level students.

Purpose


  1. Design a machine (manual control) which is capable to fight with opponent in the arena. And the destruction is the only way to show the power; here the robot who shows the power will be king of the arena.

Rules & Regulations


  • Machine Specifications:

  • The machine should fit within a box of dimensions 50*50*50(L*B*H) cm

  • The Machine should not weight more than 10Kg.

  • Both wireless and wired machines are allowed.

  • Use of readymade Logo kits is not allowed.

  • Participants will be allowed to use maximum 24V DC power supply.

  • The external device used to control the machine or any external tank is not included in the size constraint.
