Eligibility Criteria


The contest is open for B.E/B. Tech and Polytechnic level students.

Purpose


  1. Treasure hunt is an event which poses a series of questions on the paper as challenges to the participants. When the participant solves each and every question in the series, they will reach the final destination where there is a treasure.

Rules & Regulations


  • You have to only use provided material. For any malpractices will result in disqualification.

  • A group shall not consist of more than 6 participants.

  • Use of mobile phones or any other electronic equipment during the competition is not permitted.

  • All participants are required to report 30 min. before the event starts, to the reporting desk.

  • Every participant should have his college identity card or at least one proof of identity. All the teams are required to bring the registration receipt with them.

  • In case of inconvenience participants are required to seek any of the volunteers present.

  • AAny misbehavior of participant during event may lead to eliminate from the event he/she is currently in.

  • All the team members must be present during the event at the specified time. If the team is absent, it will be disqualified.

  • No extra time will be given to any team under any circumstances. Decision of the judges is final.

  • Organizers are not responsible for any loss in property, placed clues, injury and delays caused by participants during the event.

  • Participants are required to stay up to date with website.

  • All the rights are reserved with IGNITE-2023 committee.

  • Only 10 teams will be selected.
